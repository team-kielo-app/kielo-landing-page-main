## The Ritual of the Vihta or Vasta

A vital part of the sauna ritual is the *vihta* (or *vasta* in eastern Finland), a bundle of birch branches used to gently whip the skin. This practice improves circulation, cleanses the pores, and releases a soothing aroma. The *vihta* is typically prepared in the summer and dried for use throughout the year. The rhythmic strokes of the *vihta* against the skin are both invigorating and relaxing, adding another layer to the multi-sensory sauna experience.
