## The Importance of Punctuality and Communication

Finns highly value punctuality. Arriving on time shows respect for your host's schedule. It's also polite to inform your host of your estimated arrival time beforehand. If you are delayed, notify them as soon as possible. Clear communication is key to a good visit, whether it's confirming the arrangements or letting them know about any dietary restrictions you may have. A simple phone call or message to confirm your arrival details is greatly appreciated.

## Shoe-Free Zone: A Finnish Tradition
